Guide to ITR Filing for Business Owners

March 5, 2025 ITR

Understanding ITR Forms

Selecting the right ITR form is crucial for accurate tax filing. Here's a comprehensive guide to help you choose:

  • ITR-1 (Sahaj): For salaried individuals with income up to ₹50 lakhs
  • ITR-2: For individuals and HUFs with capital gains
  • ITR-3: For business owners and professionals
  • ITR-4 (Sugam): For presumptive income from business

Required Documents

Gather these essential documents before starting your ITR filing:

  • Income Documents:
    • Form 16 (if salaried)
    • Bank statements
    • Investment proofs
    • Business account books
  • Deduction Proofs:
    • Section 80C investments
    • Health insurance premiums
    • Home loan statements
    • Donation receipts
  • Business Documents:
    • GST returns
    • Balance sheet
    • Profit & Loss statement
    • Tax audit report (if applicable)

Key Deductions for Business Owners

Maximize your tax savings with these important deductions:

  • Section 80C (₹1.5 lakhs):
    • PPF, ELSS, Life Insurance
    • Employee's PF contribution
    • Fixed Deposits (5-year lock-in)
  • Business Expenses:
    • Rent and utilities
    • Employee salaries
    • Depreciation on assets
    • Professional development
  • Additional Deductions:
    • Section 80D: Health Insurance
    • Section 80GG: House Rent
    • Section 80JJA: Employment Generation

Filing Process

Follow these steps for a smooth ITR filing experience:

  1. Register on the Income Tax portal
  2. Choose the correct ITR form
  3. Fill in personal and income details
  4. Claim appropriate deductions
  5. Pay taxes (if any)
  6. Verify your return

Important Deadlines

Mark these crucial dates in your calendar:

  • Regular Filing: July 31, 2025
  • Tax Audit Cases: October 31, 2025
  • Revised Return: December 31, 2025
  • Belated Return: March 31, 2026
