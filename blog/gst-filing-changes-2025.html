Important Changes in GST Filing for FY 2025-26

March 15, 2025 GST

Key Updates in GST Filing Process

As we step into the financial year 2025-26, the GST framework has undergone significant enhancements to streamline the filing process. These changes aim to reduce compliance burden while ensuring greater transparency and accuracy in tax reporting.

New Digital Verification System

The GST portal has implemented an advanced two-factor authentication system for enhanced security. Key features include:

  • Biometric verification option for high-value transactions
  • AI-powered document verification to reduce processing time
  • Real-time validation of input tax credit claims
  • Automated reconciliation with e-way bills

Updated Filing Deadlines

Mark your calendar with these crucial deadlines:

  • GSTR-1: 11th of every month
  • GSTR-3B: 20th of every month
  • Annual Return: December 31, 2025
  • Reconciliation Statement: January 31, 2026

Simplified Input Tax Credit Claims

The process for claiming input tax credits has been streamlined with these improvements:

  • Auto-population of ITC details from supplier returns
  • Monthly ITC reconciliation reports
  • Simplified correction mechanism for mismatched credits
  • Enhanced tracking of provisional credits

Penalties and Late Fees

Stay compliant to avoid these penalties:

  • Late filing fees: ₹100 per day per Act
  • Interest on delayed payment: 18% per annum
  • ITC mismatch penalty: Up to 10% of the difference
